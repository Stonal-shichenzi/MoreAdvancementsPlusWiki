More Advancements Plus

标签

More Advancements Plus新增了一些标签。

物品标签

#madv_plus:colourful_blocks

含有#minecraft:concrete、#minecraft:concrete_powders、#minecraft:terracotta与#minecraft:glazed_terracotta。

#madv_plus:dirt_increment

含有#madv_plus:dirt_plus、#minecraft:mud与minecraft:packed_mud。该标签未使用。

#madv_plus:dirt_plus

含有#minecraft:dirt、minecraft:podzol与minecraft:grass_block。

#madv_plus:ores

含有#minecraft:coal_ores、#minecraft:lapis_ores、#minecraft:redstone_ores、#minecraft:diamond_ores、#minecraft:emerald_ores、#minecraft:gold_ores、#minecraft:iron_ores、#minecraft:copper_ores与minecraft:ancient_debris。

#madv_plus:ores_blast_block

含有minecraft:coal_block、minecraft:lapis_block、minecraft:redstone_block、minecraft:diamond_block、minecraft:emerald_block、minecraft:gold_block、minecraft:iron_block、minecraft:copper_block与minecraft:netherite_block。

#madv_plus:ores_plus

含有#madv_plus:ores与#madv_plus:ores_blast_block。

#madv_plus:wood

含有#minecraft:logs、#minecraft:planks、minecraft:bamboo_block、minecraft:bamboo_mosaic与minecraft:stripped_bamboo_block。
